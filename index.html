Privacy Policy

MusicMemo Pro

Last updated: April 30, 2026

MusicMemo Pro is designed with your privacy in mind. This app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

MusicMemo Pro does not collect any personal information. All your recordings, notes, and data are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Data Storage

Your audio recordings and app data are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Audio Recording

When you use the microphone to record audio, all recordings are saved locally on your device. The app does not transmit audio data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

MusicMemo Pro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Cloud Backup

If you choose to use the encrypted backup feature, your backup files remain stored locally or in your chosen cloud storage. We do not have access to your backups or encryption password.

Permissions

MusicMemo Pro may request the following device permissions:

  • Microphone — Required for audio recording
  • Local Network — Optional, used only for P2P sharing between your own devices

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
