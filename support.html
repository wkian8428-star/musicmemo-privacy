Common Questions

How do I record audio?

Tap the red record button on the Record tab. Tap again to stop recording. Your memo is saved automatically.

How do I export my recordings?

Open any recording, tap the share button, and choose your preferred format (M4A, WAV, or MIDI).

How does encrypted backup work?

Go to Settings → Backup → Export. Enter a password to encrypt your backup. To restore, use the Import option with the same password.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! MusicMemo Pro works entirely offline. All recordings and data are stored locally on your device.

What languages are supported?

MusicMemo Pro supports 9 languages: English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, 日本語, 한국어, Deutsch, Español, Français, Português (Brasil).
